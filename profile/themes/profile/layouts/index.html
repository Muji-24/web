Our Hypothesis

The digital landscape of Pakistan, a developing country, has three major issues:

  • It majorly relies on softwares and technology produced by developed Countries
  • It needs to adopt practices and technologies marketed by developed countries without being able to analyze its pros and cons for our society, and deviance from Islamic Values
  • Our adoption and reliance on these technogies is constantly increasing, which can potentially risk freedom and self-sustainability of our future generations

To solve these problems, we plan to take following three major steps:

  • Create digital awarness in our youth in such a way that they become real contributors to society in earlier age.
  • Produce open-source technologies through collaboration of industrialists, educationists, technologiests, and learners for enabling localized digital innovation
  • Critically analyze different technologies through thorough studies and experiments so that we can identify it's impacts, potential risks and deviation of islamic values

Our Values

Inspired by the concept of 5-Pillars of Islam, we have defined following 5 values centered around practical and measureable approach of Islamic 5-Pillars. If Allah wills, 5-Pillars of Islam and these 5 values will help us stay true to our mission in the name of Allah.

Faith Based:

Submit to the will of Allah

Seeking new knowledge is a very challenging and slow process. It takes years and even generations to produce real outcomes. Hence, it would be very important to accept the success and failures as a blessing from Allah. We need to keep praying, keep learning, keep trying and keey asking for forgiveness. It's only Allah's mercy that can keep us on the right path.

Daily Act of Seeking Guidance:

Daily Quran Recitation with Understanding and Analysis

As we want to accumulate knowledge that can benefit our Muslim community and the Humanity, then There is no better source of knowledge other than Quran. Hence, by the help of Allah, we will be pursing the habbit of daily quran recitation with proper understanding and critical reflection
