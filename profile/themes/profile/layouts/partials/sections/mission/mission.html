Our Hypothesis

The digital landscape of Pakistan, a developing country, has three major issues:

  • It majorly relies on softwares and technology produced by developed Countries
  • It needs to adopt practices and technologies marketed by developed countries without being able to analyze its pros and cons for our society, and deviance from Islamic Values
  • Our adoption and reliance on these technogies is constantly increasing, which can potentially risk freedom and self-sustainability of our future generations

To solve these problems, we plan to take following three major steps:

  • Create digital awarness in our youth in such a way that they become real contributors to society in earlier age.
  • Produce open-source technologies through collaboration of industrialists, educationists, technologiests, and learners for enabling localized digital innovation
  • Critically analyze different technologies through thorough studies and experiments so that we can identify it's impacts, potential risks and deviation of islamic values
